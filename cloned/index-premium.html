01 / About

Built in India.
Built to last.

Since 1997, Camstar Defence has stood for uncompromising precision in Indian firearms manufacturing.

Founded in Kanpur — India's historic arms manufacturing hub — Camstar Defence has grown from a regional manufacturer into one of the most respected names in Indian precision firearms. Our factory operates under the strictest compliance frameworks, with every firearm individually tested before dispatch.

We serve licensed individual owners, sport shooting associations, private security operators and government-approved dealers across the country. Our training facility in Kanpur hosts certified marksmanship programmes year-round.

From the classic .30 bore platform to our modern .45 ACP competition pistols, every Camstar sidearm carries 27 years of engineering refinement. Steel-on-steel lockup, hand-fitted components, and a finish that endures thousands of rounds.

02 / Why Camstar

Engineering first.
Always.

Four pillars that define every firearm that leaves our factory floor.

Precision Machining

CNC-machined components held to tolerances of ±0.01mm. Steel-on-steel lockup for zero-play action every cycle.

Multi-Layer Safety

Up to three independent safety mechanisms on defence-grade models. Ambidextrous controls on select platforms.

Field Reliability

Every model endures 5,000-round proof testing before production approval. Designed for India's demanding climate and conditions.

Competition Proven

Used in national-level sport shooting competitions. Preferred by ISSF-affiliated clubs and marksmanship academies across India.
